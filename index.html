The Process

Here's How It works!

You send your requirements

We receive your requirements such as team size, tentative budget and dates so that we can make a proposal tailor-made for your needs.

You get a detailed proposal

We provide all the comprehensive details such as pricing, a list of activities, functioning process and available slots to help you arrive at a decision without any confusion.

We fix a date and time

Once you pick the set of activities, we lock the date and time based on your convenience. We will send some teaser posters to hype up the event and excite your team for the big day.

Get ready with your backpacks

The day is finally here. Relax, forget everything about work for a while and have loads of fun while our facilitators take care of the hosting part.
